** Favorite pairings: Snily, Honks

Favorite fic overall: linkffn(The Peace Not Promised) This is the fic that made me like the ship. In hindsight, an unique twist in the (very common) SS Peggy Sue in that it drops him /after/ the events that lost him his friend, and rather than basically turning him into a pseudo-Gryffindor, has him try to show Slytherins an alternative path. I'd have preferred the reconcilation to take longer though, with a bigger focus on Slytherin House politics. As a sidenote, I like the alchemy lessons and would like to see more unique lessons in subjects in other fics, something I very rarely see.

Other fics that stand out for various pairings -- not necessarily my favorite fics, but fics I liked:

Hinny: linkffn(Backwards with Purpose) The second fic I ever read (after HPMoR which I found mediocre) and the fic that got me into HP fanfiction.

Haphne: linkffn(Harry Potter and the Importance of Intent) The most recent fic I read out of these, and the Haphne fic I like the most. Most Haphne fics make Daphne basically a gateway to Lord Hadrian-whatever politics silliness, this one does something different.

HPBB: linkffn(Delenda Est) I like the first half more than the second.

Honks: linkffn(Like Grains of Sand in the Hourglass) This is the fic that made HPNT one of the pairings I like the most. Unfortunately, every other fic I've seen with it goes something like this -- "omg, you asked for my true form, let's love each other forever".
