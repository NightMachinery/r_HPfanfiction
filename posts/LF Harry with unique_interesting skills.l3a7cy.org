I'm looking for some reasonably long (>60k) fanfictions where Harry has some interesting skills or abilities. Preferably set in Pre-Hogwarts or Hogwarts time. Also preferably not Dark Harry but grey-ish is fine. Some examples of what I'm talking about are:

linkffn(7619993) - Calculation

linkffn(5782108) - Methods of Rationality

linkffn(12461030) - The Tinkerer

Also, fics where Harry is a metamorphagus or young animagus or something like that are cool as well.


** The Accidental Animagus: linkao3(14078862) Harry becomes an Animagus at age 5, and learns a little wandless magic before he goes to Hogwarts. Fair warning: It does make use of the "11 year old politicians" tropes, but it's one of the few fics that hasn't completely turned me off of reading with them.

We, Harry Potter has... Well. You don't get much more interesting as an ability than "permanently turn into a multi-headed dragon". It's not at all like your examples, but it definitely qualifies for "Harry with interesting abilities", and given the last sentence of the request I think you might like it. linkffn(12610360)

Victoria Potter by Taure is a fem!Harry with a significant talent for magic in general, and Metamorphmagus abilities: linkao3(13795605)

If you're okay with another person being the protagonist, there's The Arithmancer: linkao3(10070079) Hermione is an Arithmancy prodigy in this one.
