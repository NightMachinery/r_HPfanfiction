* Survival/Strange worlds, where the setting almost doesn't even feel like the HP world anymore.
:PROPERTIES:
:Author: Edin19
:Score: 15
:DateUnix: 1540089955.0
:DateShort: 2018-Oct-21
:FlairText: Request
:END:
Worthy of Magic is probably the best example when it comes to survival fics, where unlikely allies such as Moody and Voldemort end up working together to survive in Harry's mad world. Hogwarts Battle School by Kwan Li to a lesser extent. Elizium for Sleepless Souls is also brilliant when it comes to this, and Realignment by Puzzled where Tom Riddle and Dumbledore team up, though i can't remember any more of these type of fics. Would like fics close to the level of those mentioned above if possible, for those of you who have read them.

Regarding the strange worlds request, Worthy of Magic also applies to that, as well as fics like The Song of the Trees by Tinn Tam. Both are fics where the world feels strangely removed from that of HP canon once you dive deeper into both fics, with how much the world changes, despite neither of them being crossovers. So if any fics have ever made you feel that way, please recommend them :)
