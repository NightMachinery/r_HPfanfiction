** The first fic I ever read was also the best, so it's like my gold standard - Memories and Dreams, a series by Paganaidd, that's on AO3, and FFN. It's after the 7 books, deals with Harry reuniting with Dudley and adopting a young wizard boy named Tim who was brought up in an abusive home. It's perfection, despite elements I usually frown upon like supernatural stuff (I mean besides the magic which is just a part of the world). The characters are true to themselves in the books... but also change reliably as the books take place in Harry's middle age years. I cannot recommend it enough.

** I rec the following a lot, but I also reread them.

There is the whole Dangerverse starting with linkffn(Living With Danger). I love Harry raised by others, which initially drew me to this fic. The OCs actually have flaws. There are a lot of main Hogwarts kids Characters, but they all get some level of character arc eventually. It covers pre-Hogwarts and Hogwarts years so it can bog in about year 4, but I think the series is worth sticking with. The further along it goes, the further it gets from stations of cannon, and the better the world-building gets.

There is also [[https://www.tthfanfic.org/Story-30822/DianeCastle+Hermione+Granger+and+the+Boy+Who+Lived.htm][Hermione Granger and the Boy Who Lived]], which is a different take even for an AU. It gets a little wordy sometimes, and the names are slightly off (which my brain ignores), but I love it for how unique it is, it's good characterization of Ron and its world-building.
