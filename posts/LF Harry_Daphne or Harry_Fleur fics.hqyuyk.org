** My two favorite pairings.

Most of my favorite fics with them are unfortunately not completed(The Stag and The Flower for example) but here are those that are.

Well for Harry/Fleur there is:

Letters linkffn(6535391) which was the fic that started me on this ship.

A Cadmean Victory linkffin(11446957) A classic and in which the Romance is fairly good.

Euphoria linkffn(11534019) a very good example of canon-compliant romance without any artifice(soul bond, veela bond, super!Harry)

Tri-Wizard Champions linkffn(6531771) I remember liking this one, but can't remember the plot.

For Harry/Daphne:

The Legacy Preservation Act linkffn(10649604) a bit cliched, but the author James Spookie has a lot of stories with this pairing, the Lion Tamer trilogy comes to mind.

Honor Thy Blood linkffn(12155794) a powerful Harry that falls in love with her(I don't remember how)

From Southhampton to New York and Beyond linkffn(12300252) Harry meets Daphne in a cruise post-war and they fall in love, IIRC it has some Weasley bashing(Ron and Ginny). But the author Dorothea Greengrass, as you can guess by the name, has a lot of stories with the pairing.

Novocaine linkffn(13022013) Harry gets the opportunity to start rebuilding the world after the war and hires Daphne as his lawyer, obviously, they fall in love.

Daphne Greengrass and The Importance of Intent linkffn(13133746) a good one too.

A Champion's New Hope linkffn(5244813) I have seen this one recommended a few times, but I've never read too much of it.
